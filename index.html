Arduinocarolers

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

In this project, we programmed three Arduinos to butcher Christmas songs in three-part harmony.

The arduinos receive an infra-red signal from a TV remote control. This ensures they are synchronised.

The key pressed on the remote control determines which of the three songs is played:

  • Jingle Bells
  • We Wish You a Merry Christmas
  • Christmas Is Coming (that favourite from the back-catalog of The Muppets and John Denver!)
